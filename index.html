Welcome to the official domain of the Commission on Membership. For years, we have stood as guardians of honor, unity, and service. Our kingdom is not built on power alone, but on the unbreakable bond of loyalty among its people. Here, every member carries the weight of tradition and the promise of a brighter future for all who seek purpose and belonging.

About the Kingdom

Our Heritage

The Sovereign Kingdom of Dahlia was founded on the timeless principles of honor, justice, and unity. Every decree, every gathering, and every member represents a vital chapter in our continuing legacy.

Our Values

At the heart of Dahlia lie three unshakable pillars that guide every decision:

  • Honor: We act with integrity in all things.
  • Loyalty: We stand together, no member left behind.
  • Service: We dedicate ourselves to our community.

Our Vision

We envision a kingdom where tradition and innovation walk hand in hand. Where every voice is heard, every member is valued, and every generation leaves the realm stronger than they found it.
